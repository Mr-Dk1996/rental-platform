Help & Support

This page guides tenants, landlords, and administrators on how to use RentHaven Ghana. Use the sections below to understand how to create an account, browse properties, upload listings, negotiate, and manage your account.

Creating an Account

  • Click Sign Up on the homepage.
  • Choose Tenant or Landlord.
  • Enter your name, email, password, and security answer.
  • You can also continue with Google as Tenant or Landlord.

For Tenants

  • Browse available rooms and apartments.
  • Use search and filters to narrow results.
  • Save spaces you like for later.
  • Open property details and send an offer.
  • Use Smart AI Match for recommendations.

For Landlords

  • Add property details and upload images.
  • Manage your listed properties.
  • Receive tenant offers.
  • Accept or reject negotiations.
  • Track active leases after accepting an offer.

For Admin

  • Monitor total users and platform activity.
  • View and filter users.
  • View and moderate property listings.
  • Monitor negotiations and active leases.
  • Generate printable system reports.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a tenant save properties?

Yes. Tenants can save properties using the heart/save button. Saved spaces can be viewed later from the Saved Spaces section.

Can a landlord upload more than one property?

Yes. A landlord can add and manage multiple properties from the landlord dashboard.

What happens when a landlord accepts an offer?

The property status changes to Occupied, and the accepted negotiation becomes an active lease record.

Can users update their profile?

Yes. Users can update profile information such as full name, phone numbers, email request, and password from their account settings.

What does Smart AI Match do?

Smart AI Match helps tenants find suitable properties by scoring available listings using preferences, saved spaces, negotiation history, location, budget, and property type.

Still need help?

Go back to the homepage and log in to your account. You can update your profile, check notifications, and continue using the dashboard features.
