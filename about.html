About the platform

What is RentHaven Ghana?

RentHaven Ghana connects tenants directly with landlords. Tenants can browse available spaces, view property details, send offers, save preferred rooms, and manage accepted leases. Landlords can list properties, upload images, receive offers, respond to negotiations, and manage their listings.

Simple Property Search

Tenants can search by location, property type, price range, and availability.

Direct Negotiation

Tenants can send offers directly to landlords and track responses from the dashboard.

Smart AI Match

The platform recommends suitable properties using preferences, saved spaces, and activity.

AI Chatbot Help

Users can get instant help, ask basic questions, and search for available properties using the floating AI assistant.

Our Purpose

RentHaven Ghana was created to reduce the stress tenants face when searching for rooms and to help landlords manage their property listings more effectively. Instead of depending only on phone calls, scattered adverts, agents, and manual follow-ups, the platform provides a centralized place for rental activities.

Why It Matters

Finding accommodation can be difficult when information is scattered, prices are unclear, and communication is not properly organized. RentHaven Ghana improves this process by making rental information easier to access, compare, manage, and monitor.

How it works

A simple rental process

The system follows a clear process from property search to accepted lease.

1

Browse properties

Tenants view available spaces and filter by location, type, and budget.

2

View details

Tenants open a property to view images, specifications, landlord details, and description.

3

Send offer

The tenant sends an offer amount and message directly to the landlord.

4

Track lease

When accepted, the property becomes occupied and the lease appears in the tenant dashboard.

What the System Provides

  • Tenant and landlord account registration with role-based dashboards.
  • Property upload and management for landlords.
  • Browse, search, filter, and save spaces for tenants.
  • Offer submission, negotiation tracking, and chat support.
  • Landlord public profile with other available properties.
  • Smart AI-assisted property matching and floating search assistant.
  • Instant AI chatbot support for property search and platform guidance.
