Need help using RentHaven Ghana?

RentHaven Ghana provides support for tenants and landlords who need help creating an account, searching for properties, uploading listings, sending offers, or understanding how the platform works.

Account Support

  • Create a Tenant or Landlord account from the homepage.
  • Use email/password or Google sign-up.
  • Update your profile and phone numbers from account settings.

Tenant Support

  • Browse available rooms and apartments.
  • Search by location, property type, or price range.
  • Save spaces, send offers, and track negotiations.

Landlord Support

  • Add property details and upload multiple images.
  • Manage available, occupied, and inactive listings.
  • Review tenant offers and manage active leases.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click Log In / Sign Up from the homepage, choose Sign Up, enter your details, and select whether you are a Tenant or Landlord.

How can tenants contact landlords?

Tenants can open a property details page, view the landlord contact number, or send an offer with a message to begin negotiation.

Can landlords upload multiple properties?

Yes. Landlords can add and manage more than one property from the landlord dashboard.

What happens when an offer is accepted?

The property is marked as occupied, and the accepted negotiation becomes an active lease record for the tenant and landlord.

What does Smart AI Match do?

Smart AI Match helps tenants find suitable properties using preferences such as location, property type, budget, saved spaces, and negotiation activity.
